About

I am Patrick Stewart, a wildlife and nature photographer from Dundee, Scotland. My career as a photographer started as a hobby, taking photos of animals and landscapes while travelling the world. After a handful of trips I realised I had amassed a wealth of photos from and began to post my work online.

Eventually I was approached by the RSPB to help with a project documenting Scottish birds. This kickstarted my career as a professional photographer. I have and continue to work with charities in Scotland and around the world. I also work with marine biology researchers in Tayside and St. Andrews documenting dolphins in north east Scotland.
